# Project Overview: Tayyibt

**Tayyibt** is an AI-powered smart matchmaking and marriage platform specifically designed for Muslim communities worldwide. It aims to facilitate meaningful connections based on shared religious values, lifestyle choices, and personal interests, providing a secure and culturally appropriate environment for those seeking marriage.

## Mission
To provide a technologically advanced yet religiously sensitive platform that helps Muslims find their ideal life partners through intelligent matching and robust social features.

## Target Audience
- Single Muslims seeking marriage.
- Guardians (Walis) involved in the matchmaking process.
- Community agents and matchmakers.

## Platform Components

| Component | Technology | Purpose |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| **Backend API** | NestJS (Node.js) | Core business logic, data management, and real-time communication. |
| **Web App** | Next.js (React) | Primary user interface for web browsers. |
| **Mobile App** | Flutter | Native mobile experience for iOS and Android. |
| **Admin Dashboard** | Next.js (React) | Internal tool for user moderation, content management, and analytics. |
| **AI Service** | FastAPI (Python) | Specialized service for calculating compatibility scores and match ranking. |

## Core Values
- **Privacy & Security:** Strong emphasis on data protection and secure communication.
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Designed with Islamic values and traditions at its core.
- **Intelligence:** Leveraging AI to improve the quality and relevance of matches.
- **Community:** Beyond just matching, fostering a sense of community through groups and social feeds.
