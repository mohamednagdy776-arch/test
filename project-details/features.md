# Features and Modules

Tayyibt is a comprehensive platform with a wide array of features designed to support the journey from discovery to marriage.

## 1. Authentication & Profile
- **Multi-method Login:** Email and Phone-based authentication.
- **Detailed Profiles:** Religious commitment, sect, lifestyle, education, occupation, and more.
- **Avatar Management:** Secure image uploads for profile pictures.
- **User Search:** Advanced filtering by location, age, sect, and other preferences.

## 2. Smart Matching
- **AI-Powered Scores:** Compatibility scores based on a multi-factor algorithm.
- **Match Workflow:** Ability to accept or reject matches.
- **Match Reasons:** Transparency on why two users were paired.

## 3. Real-time Communication
- **Private Chat:** One-on-one encrypted messaging for accepted matches.
- **Chat Rooms:** Organized by matches.
- **Media Sharing:** Support for sending images and videos (planned/implemented).

## 4. Community & Social Feed
- **Groups:** Join or create groups based on interests or community.
- **Social Feed:** Post updates, share media, and interact with others' content.
- **Comments & Reactions:** Engage with posts in the community feed.

## 5. Notifications
- **In-app Notifications:** Real-time updates for new matches, messages, and social interactions.
- **Unread Counters:** Clear visibility of pending actions.

## 6. Subscriptions & Monetization
- **Tiered Plans:** Free and Premium subscription models.
- **Payment Integration:** Secure transaction handling for subscriptions.
- **Affiliate Program:** Referral system to reward users for bringing others to the platform.

## 7. Moderation & Safety
- **Report System:** Users can report inappropriate content or behavior.
- **Admin Controls:** Dashboard for banning/unbanning users and resolving reports.
- **Content Moderation:** Ensuring all community content aligns with platform values.

## 8. Internationalization
- **Multi-language Support:** Designed for global Muslim communities.
- **RTL Support:** Native handling of Right-to-Left languages like Arabic and Urdu.
